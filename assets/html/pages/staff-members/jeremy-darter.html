Jeremy Darter

Director of Business Development

Jeremy Darter currently serves as the Director of Business Development for SALUS TeleHealth. In this role, he leads all sales and marketing efforts for the organization. Jeremy previously has held positions with Newell Rubbermaid and Forest Laboratories where he gained extensive experience in healthcare, pharmaceutical sales and marketing management. He is a graduate of Texas Tech University and married to Kelli Willemssen Darter. They have one son, Noah.
