Dr. Jeffrey Kesler

President & Chief Operating Officer

Dr. Jeffrey Kesler is serves as the Chief Operating Officer for SALUS TeleHealth. Global Partinership for TeleHealth consists of five companies responsible for the advancement of telehealth with a mission to deliver worldwide access to healthcare. Dr. Kesler has over 20 years of broad clinical and operational experience in the healthcare field. He has been responsible for strategic and tactical leadership for large healthcare companies. Dr. Kesler has served as the executive lead for the development and implementation of telemedicine programs in many states and numerous facilities throughout the United States. Outcomes associated with his implementation of telemedicine programs have resulted in improved clinical outcomes, increased access to care, and significant cost savings. He is a nationally recognized speaker on the design, implementation, operation, and administration of telehealth programs.
